Introduction

Welcome to Sudoku Master. This Privacy Policy explains how Multi Tech Innovations ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By downloading or using Sudoku Master you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

02

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Sudoku Master does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information directly from you.

Information Collected Automatically

The following data may be collected automatically when you use the App:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • Gameplay data (grid size, difficulty, levels completed, hints used, time spent)
  • App usage events (screens visited, features tapped, theme preferences)
  • Ad interaction data (ad shown, skipped, or rewarded)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data
03

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the App and save your game progress locally on your device
  • To improve gameplay, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob (see Section 4)
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations
🔒 We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
04

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Sudoku Master uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Ad types shown in this App:

✅ Rewarded ads (for extra hints) ✅ Rewarded interstitials (between levels) ✅ App open ads (on launch) 🚫 No banner ads

Google AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including Advertising ID (GAID / IDFA), IP address, and device signals. This is governed by the Google Privacy Policy ↗.

Opt Out of Personalized Ads

🤖 Android

Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

🍎 iOS

Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable ad tracking

05

Data Storage & Retention

Local Storage (On Your Device)

Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard Android/iOS shared preferences. This data never leaves your device unless transmitted by third-party SDKs described in this policy.

  • Current saved game state
  • Levels completed and star ratings
  • Total play time and hints used
  • Theme, sound, and vibration preferences
  • Last-used grid size and difficulty

Third-Party Data Retention

Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's own data retention policies. We do not control this data once transmitted to Google.

06

Analytics

Sudoku Master uses lightweight local analytics for development diagnostics. In the current version, analytics events (level starts, completions, hint usage) are logged only to the device console in debug mode and are not transmitted to any external server.

⚠️ If a third-party analytics platform is integrated in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated before that version is released.
07

Children's Privacy

Sudoku Master is a general-audience puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us (see Section 12) and we will promptly delete such information.

Advertising served through AdMob is configured in accordance with Google's policies on child-directed content.

08

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information associated with your use of the App. Game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's own security features.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12.
